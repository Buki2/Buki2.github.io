Yuqi Bu

Ph.D. Student, South China University of Technology.

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Key Laboratory of Big Data and Intelligent Robot at South China University of Technology (SCUT), advised by Prof. Yi Cai and Prof. Qiong Liu. Additionally, I am currently on an academic visit to the School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS).

My research interests revolve around multimodal learning, human-robot collaboration, and, more specifically, visual grounding.

Feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research work or if you are seeking potential collaborations. [Email]
